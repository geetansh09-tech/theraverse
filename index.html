    Why we exist

    A brand built to bridge the gap

    Mental healthcare in India is fragmented — expensive therapy on one end, silence and stigma on the other. Theraverse sits in between.

    Our Vision

    To become India's biggest mental health support brand — a name every household trusts, the way they trust a family doctor, but for the mind.

    Our Mission

    We exist to:

    • Bridge the gap between people and the decision to seek therapy
    • Remove the stigma around talking about mental health, openly and in Indian languages people actually speak
    • Make self-healing tools free and accessible to anyone with a phone
    • Let licensed psychologists earn fairly for the resources they create
